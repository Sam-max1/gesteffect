# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

GestEffect is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for all participants, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being patient and understanding with others
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy and gratitude
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing
- Listening to different viewpoints

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or bullying
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Offensive or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing others' private information
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention
- Other conduct that violates applicable laws

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards and enforcing this code fairly.

### Reporting
Report violations to the project maintainers via private message or email. Include:
- Description of the violation
- Time and place it occurred
- Any witnesses or evidence

### Response
The team will investigate all reports and respond appropriately. We are committed to:
- Taking all reports seriously
- Maintaining confidentiality
- Being fair and impartial
- Following up with all parties

### Consequences
Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and community interactions.
